## 📖 Project Overview

Designed and independently implemented a longitudinal REDCap clinical research database supporting standardized electronic case report forms (eCRFs), longitudinal participant follow-up, and structured clinical data management.

The project incorporates structured electronic Case Report Forms (eCRFs), longitudinal event mapping, branching logic, calculated fields, validation rules, and standardized clinical measurements to ensure accurate, consistent, and efficient data collection.

The study follows participants across four scheduled visits, capturing demographic information, medical history, anthropometric measurements, blood pressure, laboratory results, and educational interventions within a centralized REDCap environment.

This project was designed and implemented to create a standardized longitudinal clinical research database for REDCap.

## 🧪 Study Design

The project was designed as a longitudinal clinical research study consisting of four scheduled participant visits. Each visit was configured within REDCap using longitudinal events and assigned electronic Case Report Forms (eCRFs) to ensure standardized and consistent data collection throughout the study.

### Study Visits

| Visit | Data Collected |
|--------|----------------|
| **Visit 1 – Baseline** | Screening & Informed Consent, Demographics, Social Determinants of Health, Primary Care Provider Information, Medical History, Anthropometric Measurements, Blood Pressure, Lipid Panel, Glucose & HbA1c, Educational Materials |
| **Visit 2 – Month 1** | Anthropometric Measurements, Blood Pressure, Lipid Panel, Glucose & HbA1c, Educational Materials |
| **Visit 3 – Month 2** | Anthropometric Measurements, Blood Pressure, Lipid Panel, Glucose & HbA1c, Educational Materials |
| **Visit 4 – Month 3** | Anthropometric Measurements, Blood Pressure, Lipid Panel, Glucose & HbA1c, Educational Materials |
