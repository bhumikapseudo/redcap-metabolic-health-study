# 🏥 REDCap Metabolic Health Longitudinal Study

A REDCap-based longitudinal clinical research database designed to support metabolic health research through structured electronic case report forms (eCRFs), standardized data collection, and high-quality clinical data management.

---

## 📋 Project Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Domain** | Clinical Research & Health Informatics |
| **Platform** | REDCap |
| **Project Type** | Longitudinal Clinical Research Database |
| **Research Focus** | Metabolic Health |
| **Study Design** | Longitudinal (4 Study Visits) |
| **Electronic Forms** | 10 eCRFs |
| **Clinical Modules** | Screening, Demographics, Medical History, Clinical Measurements, Laboratory Results, Education |
| **Key Features** | Branching Logic, Validation Rules, Calculated Fields, Longitudinal Events |
